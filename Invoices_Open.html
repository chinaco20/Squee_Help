Open Invoices

Open Invoices are any invoices that still have a remaining balance after their due date, and can be bills or credits. Bills may require recalculation of Lost Earnings before a follow up (2nd Notice).

View summary of Open Invoices for a single Employer

It cna be difficult to visualize a summary (by fund) when an empoloyer has multiple open invoices that are a mix of both bills and credits. There is an employer feature can may be able to help.

  1. Navigate to the Employer and click on the Ledger tab.

  2. Click on the 'Query for Open Invoices' button.

    • A query will open up with all open invoices grouped by invoice and segretated by fund.
    • You can copy and past the query results to a spreadsheet.
    • Don't forget to close the query once you are done.

  3. The list box on the form shows all Redeemed Credits (RC).

    • This is to help verify whether a credit is truly available or was redeemed but were unrecorded on an invoice.
