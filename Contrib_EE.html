Voluntary 401(k) Contributions

Members can contribute to their 401(k) account if they are at the 45% apprentice level or higher and have completed an ECW. Once in the program, their employers is (federaly) required to collect the correct money pre-tax deductions and send payment on their behalf. This is a weekly report.

Processing Employer Reports using Squee

Here are the steps for inputting and EE batch into Squee. Once is done Squee will upload the reports into LUMS for you.

  1. Create a new batch.

    • On the Squee main form navigator, click the Batches EE button.
    • On the Batches EE main form click the Add Batch button.
    • The Add New Batch form will pop-up. Add the batch vitals, required fields have a colored background.
      •  You can do all the batches for the day at the same time.
    • Select the target batch from the Batches listbox.

  2. Process all the employer reports in the batch.

    • Go to the Batch Reports subform and input the report vitals.
